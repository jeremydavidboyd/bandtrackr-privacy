Privacy Policy for BandTrackr

Effective Date: September 25, 2025 (Updated)

BandTrackr respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, first name, and last name.
  • Identifiers: We assign a unique user ID to manage your account.
  • Authentication Data: We securely store access and refresh tokens to keep you signed in.

We do not collect your location, contacts, device identifiers, or advertising data. We do not track usage across apps or websites.

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Authenticate logins and maintain your session.
  • Provide features available to signed-in users (e.g., submitting or editing scores where permitted).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Data Storage & Security

We store data with our cloud provider (Supabase) and use standard security practices to protect account data.

Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your account at any time by emailing bandtrackrapp@gmail.com.
  • Upon deletion, your profile data (email, name, user ID) will be permanently removed from our systems.

Children’s Privacy

BandTrackr is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and may notify users in-app.
